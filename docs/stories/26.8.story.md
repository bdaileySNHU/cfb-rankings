## Validation Results (2024 Season)

### Data Coverage
- **Total transfers fetched:** 3,378
- **With destinations:** 2,654 (78.6%)
- **With star ratings:** 2,996 (88.7%) — primary metric
- **Teams ranked:** 297

### Algorithm
Star-based points system:
| Stars | Points |
|-------|--------|
| 5★ | 100 |
| 4★ | 80 |
| 3★ | 60 |
| 2★ | 40 |
| 1★ or unrated | 20 |

Teams ranked by total points (descending); transfer count used as tiebreaker.

### Comparison vs 247Sports Top 25

| Team | 247Sports | Ours | Diff |
|------|-----------|------|------|
| Ole Miss | #1 | #3 | +2 |
| Oregon | #2 | #8 | +6 |
| Alabama | #3 | #5 | +2 |

**Spearman Rank Correlation: ~0.52** (moderate alignment)

### Key Finding: Quality vs Quantity Tradeoff

Our algorithm is **quantity-focused** — it rewards teams that bring in more
transfers regardless of star quality. 247Sports is **quality-focused** — it
rewards teams that land elite (4★/5★) transfers even if fewer in number.

**Example:** Colorado led in raw transfer volume (41 transfers, mostly 3★) and
ranked #1 in our system. 247Sports ranked them #9 because the quality was
lower despite the high volume.

### Decision: Keep Current Algorithm

The quantity-focused approach is intentional for this system because:

1. **Volume has real impact** — more transfers = more roster turnover = more
   uncertainty, which should affect ELO
2. **Data coverage** — stars have 88.7% coverage vs 54.8% for ratings; star
   points are reliable
3. **Transparency** — simple sum of points is easy to explain and audit
4. **Sufficient correlation** — 0.52 Spearman shows reasonable alignment;
   the differences are explainable by methodology, not data errors

The frontend tooltip on team pages explicitly notes: *"Volume-based ranking:
Rewards teams with more transfers. For quality-weighted rankings, see 247Sports."*

## Validation Utility

The comparison script is available at `utilities/compare_transfer_rankings.py`.
It reads live rankings from the database and computes the Spearman correlation
against the hardcoded 247Sports Top 25. Run it after any re-import to verify
rankings remain consistent:

```bash
python utilities/compare_transfer_rankings.py
```
