How It Works

ELO Predictions

Our ELO system generates predictions based on team ratings, strength of schedule, and home field advantage. Higher-rated teams are predicted to beat lower-rated teams.

  • Objective mathematical model
  • Accounts for opponent strength
  • Win probability calculations
  • Updates continuously

AP Poll "Predictions"

The AP Poll doesn't make explicit predictions, but we can infer: if Team A is ranked higher than Team B, the AP Poll "predicts" Team A should win.

  • Based on voter opinions
  • Higher rank = predicted winner
  • Unranked teams not compared
  • Updated weekly
